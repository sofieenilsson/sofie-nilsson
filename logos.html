Bravikens Sågverk

A suggestion for a brand identity for Bravikens Sågverk (sawmill) in Norrköping, Sweden. Includes logo in color and b/w, and a pattern based on the icon in the logotype.

Bravikens Sågverk are Scandinavia's biggest and most modern sawmill and it produces spruce construction timber. As up until today they didn't have their own graphic profile. As they are the biggest player in Scandinavia, when it comes to sawmills, they most definitely deserve to have a graphic profile that reflects and captivates both their modern approch and their history.

Cookery

Cookery is an online platform where private and unemployed chefs can offer their services to people. Regardless of if you're hosting a wedding or a small cozy dinner for your friends, you'll find the perfect chef with the Cookery platform.

Sonocube

A Hyper Island student project during the module Exploring Technology with industry leader Måns Adler from the global digital product studio UsTwo.

The Sonocube is a light and sound installation and can be referred to as a modern version of the classic jukebox. It consists of a big wooden box, metal pipes and LED-lights. By touching one of the metal pipes the user themselves can decide what song to be played.
