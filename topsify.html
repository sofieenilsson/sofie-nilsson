Topsify Cover Artwork

Topsify is a music brand with a target audience of men and women between 15-30 years old. To grow even more they want to optimize their playlist's cover artworks.

Goals:

The overall goal was to optimize Topsify's playlist’s cover arts to make them more unique and appealing to the Spotify users. This to be able to attract new users to our playlists.

End product:

Cover artwork with custom made typography.
