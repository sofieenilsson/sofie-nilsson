Get Set Go!

"Do good by being late in the morning"

Re-design of existing graphic profile and a new illustration/mascot for the Get Set Go! all girl skateboard contest (a part of Ultra Bowl in Malmö, Sweden). The logo and illustration were printed as a skateboard, t-shirts, stickers, necklaces and keychains.

End product:

Updated graphic profile and an illustrated mascot.
