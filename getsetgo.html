Get Set Go!

Re-design of existing logotype and the creation of a new mascot

Re-design of existing logotype for the Get Set Go! all girl skateboard contest (a part of Ultra Bowl in Malmö, Sweden). To complete the new graphic profile I also created a "mascot" for the competition. This were used in print materials such as a skateboard, t-shirts, stickers, necklaces and keychains. The graphics were also used at bigscreens during the competition.

The intension with the new graphic profile for Get Set Go! is to give the competition a fun vibe. The new, crazy mascot are supposed to personify the hype the contestants feel right before they're about to dive into the competition.

End product:

Updated graphic profile and an illustrated mascot for the competition.
