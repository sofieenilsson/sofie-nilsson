Get Set Go!

Re-branding of an all girl skateboard contest

Project

Get Set Go! is an all girl skateboard contest (a part of the international skateboard contest Ultra Bowl) in Malmö, Sweden. For the 2014 years edition of the contest they needed to update their graphic profile. They were looking for a new look with both a fresh and modern feeling to it. To complete the new profile I also created a mascot which is supposed to represent the contestants and the stoke skateboarding bring them.
