Get Set Go!

Re-design of existing logotype and the creation of a new mascot

Re-design of existing logotype for the Get Set Go! all girl skateboard contest (a part of Ultra Bowl in Malmö, Sweden). To complete the new graphic profile a new "mascot" for the competition were created. This were used in print materials such as a skateboard, t-shirts, stickers, necklaces and keychains. The graphics were also used at bigscreens during the competition.

Re-branding, illustration, handlettering, merchandise
