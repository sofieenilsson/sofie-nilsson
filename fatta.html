FATTA skate

Skateboard design for the swedish organization FATTA

FATTA is a non-profit organization that fights against sexual violence. FATTA is born out of frustration at how society and the legal system looks today and it is based on over 150 anonymous peoples stories about sex that didn't happen on their own terms.
