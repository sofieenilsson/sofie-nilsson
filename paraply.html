Paraply Event & Media

Restructuring and rebranding of an umbrella organization housing three companies

Brief

Create a better company structure for Paraply Produktion and it's sub-companies and make their company communication more clear towards their customers.

Project

Paraply Produktion was founded in 2008 when a group of friends with backgrounds in live music, management, event and the advertising industry joined forces. They are a team working towards the goal of making the south east part of Sweden more attractive and active. Today the company works as an umbrella organization for a cluster of three companies housing great expertise within different areas.

Result

We got the challenge to restructure the companies and as a part of this we also decided to do some rebranding, as well as improve their online presence with some new web design. Our first step were to make it easier for both B2C and B2B customers to get a grip of what they're offering and what they can do. To do this we decided to merge the three companies into one, strong company - Paraply Event & Media.

The new graphic profile gives the company one strong and solid design which will make it easier for them to communicate clearer with their customers. We also decided to improve their online presence by giving them a brand new website. The new logotype is inspired by the old in order to let people still recognize the company. The goal here were to make all new design reflect both them and their audiences by giving it a more modern and fresh look.

Deliverables

We delivered a new brand identity with logo, colors, typefaces, and image guidelines. A brand and social media strategy. Redesign of their website and a more clear company structure for improved internal and external communcation.
