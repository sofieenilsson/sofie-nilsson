FATTA Skate

Skateboard design for the Swedish organization FATTA which is a non-profit organization that fights against sexual violence. FATTA is born out of frustration for how society and the legal system looks today and it's based on over 150 anonymous peoples stories about sex that happened without consent. The profit goes directly to the organization so that they can continue their important work.

Topsify Cover Artwork

Topsify is a music brand with a target audience of men and women between 15-30 years old. To grow even more they wanted to optimize their playlist's cover artworks. The overall goal was to optimize Topsify's playlist’s cover arts to make them more unique and appealing to the Spotify users.

This to be able to attract new users to their playlists. I wanted to give the playlists a modern and young, but at the same time playful feel to them. I made three cover artwork with custom made typography. I decided to use bright colors in order to easier attract the users attention.
