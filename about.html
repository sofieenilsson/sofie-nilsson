Hi there! My name is Sofie. I'm a designer. And a digital creative. And a brand strategist. And an illustrator. Or as we say in Sweden: "Kärt barn har många namn". Which means that a beloved child has many names. I don't really believe in giving myself only one title, so you can just call me an overall creative mind instead. I have a big fascination for colors, building strong brands and free-shaped letters of all kinds. I also like sneakers. A lot. And if you don't find me at my desk designing or drawing you'll probably find me in a nightclub living out my other passion: DJing.

Before I started at Hyper Island I studied Advertising, Graphic Design & Visual Communication at the Linnaeus University where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Media Communication. During my years at the university I started out freelancing and I've been doing that since 2013 now. My toolbox consists of the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite and my primary tool is the Adobe Illustrator CC. Although, I always start out with the simplest but yet most powerful tool of them all: the pencil.
