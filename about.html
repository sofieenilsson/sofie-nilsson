Hi there! My name is Sofie and I'm a designer from Sweden specializing in branding and illustration. I have a big fascination for building strong brands, bright colors and free-shaped letters of all kinds. I also like sneakers. A lot. If you don't find me at my desk designing or drawing you'll probably find me in a nightclub living out my other passion: DJing. And if you dare, challenge me at Super Mario 1 or 3 on the old Nintendo 8-bit. That's my hidden talent.

Before I started at Hyper Island I studied Advertising, Graphic Design & Visual Communication at the Linnaeus University where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Media Communication. During my years at the university I started out freelancing and I've been doing that since 2013 now. My toolbox consists of the Adobe Creative Cloud Suite and my primary tool is the Adobe Illustrator CC. Although, I always start out with the simplest but yet most powerful tool of them all: the pencil.

If you want to create something awesome together (or actually dare to challenge me at Super Mario), then get in touch!
