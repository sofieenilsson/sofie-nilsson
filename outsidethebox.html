Outside The Box

A survival guide for creatives

Project/Brief

We got the task to create a survival guide which helps creatives to excercise and push the boundaries of their creativity. You learn to be creative by experimenting, exploring, questioning assumptions and using your imagination. Learning to be creative requires practice to develop the right muscles. So, how can we help creatives to practice, boost and kick start their creativity?

Role: Visual Designer

Responsibilities: Concept Development, Branding, Packaging Design

Solution & result

Outside The Box is a card game (also available as a digital version) that will help you kick start your creativity. We wanted to create a product with excercises that encourages the user to think outside the box. The game consists of challenges such as writing a resume for an alien, find animal shapes in popcorn or combine two or more things to create a new invention. So, if you're feeling adventurous or just want to excercise your creative muscle, then try this game out.
