Outside The Box

A creativity game outside the box

Outside The Box is a card game (also available as a digital version) that will help you to kickstart your creativity. If you're feeling adventurous or just want to excercise your creative muscle, then try this game out. You are sure to be entertained and hopefully feel more creative afterwards. The game consists of challenges such as writing a resume for an alien, find animal shapes in popcorns or combine two or more things to create a new invention.
