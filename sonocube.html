Sonocube

A Hyper Island student project during the module Exploring Tech with industry leader Måns Adler from the global digital product studio UsTwo.

The Sonocube is a light and sound installation and can be referred to as a modern version of the classic jukebox. It consists of a big wooden box, metal pipes and LED-lights. By touching one of the metal pipes the user themselves can decide what song to be played.

We wanted to create a way for people to interact with the music in a different way than to just have a Spotify playlist running.
