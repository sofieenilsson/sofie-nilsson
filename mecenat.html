Mecenat On Campus

Promotion tour in Sweden

Mecenat On Campus 2012 is an event tour to 12 different univesitys in Sweden. During 3 weeks Mecenat meet with over 250,000 students. The concept consists of graphic materails and illustrations such as design for the event booth and printed materials.

The thought behind the branding for the concept were to combine a college feeling with the already existing Mecenat profile.
