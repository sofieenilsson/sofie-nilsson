Mecenat On Campus

Graphic material for Mecenats promotion tour in Sweden

Mecenat On Campus 2012 is an event tour to 12 different univesitys in Sweden. During 3 weeks Mecenat meet with over 250,000 students. The thought behind the branding for the concept were to combine a college feeling with the already existing Mecenat profile.

The concept consists of graphic materails and illustrations such as design for the event booth and printed materials. In order to attract the target group but at the same time maintain and strenghten the Mecenat brand the profile for the tour is very simple but yet eyecatching.
