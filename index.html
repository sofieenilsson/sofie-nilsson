I’m a designer and illustrator from Sweden with a big passion for branding. But I also like sneakers. A lot. I love creating solutions based on real life insights - real life problems to be solved for real people. The key in my process is that I believe sketching encourages original thoughts which is why I always start out analog before going digital in my process. Pen to paper and tons of post-its. I am currently attending the Digital Media Creative program at Hyper Island but I’ve done a lot of things before that. Working as a DJ for 4 years is one of them. But if you want to know more about me just drop me a line or two. I’m super friendly. I promise.
