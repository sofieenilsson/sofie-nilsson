Wake a Wish

Snooze for a good cause

Project

Wake a Wish is a very simple to use charity alarm clock application for your smart phone. By using the alarm clock application you automatically donate money to a charity organisation of your choice every time you hit the snooze button. The application gives the user an easy way to financially contributing to non-profit organisations and it doesn’t have the barriers that traditional ways of donating money often have. The idea is based on the insight that snoozing makes you feel bad. We wanted to change this behavior that often leaves the user with a feeling of guilt, into something that brings value instead. Basically, the user can do good and feel good at the same time.

The user decides and control the amount of money to donate, and every time the snooze button is pushed the money will be directly transferred to the charity organization of their choice. The Wake a Wish application seeks to challenge the traditional ways non-profit organisations gather donations today and is presenting a new revenue stream option for those organisations.

    • Design

    The brand is built upon a logo made with custom, hand drawn typography and the three brand colors black and white, with the accent color yellow. Since this is an application most likely to be used in the morning time the interface has a dark theme based on the brand colors. This in order to be gentle to the users eyes. It's also simple to use with as few user steps as possible. The first time the user opens the app they have to sign up in order to use it. The registration includes steps such as your email and payment information. After that you simply just choose the organisation/organisations you want to donate money to and set a monthly donation limit.
