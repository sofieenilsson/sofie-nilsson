Wake a Wish

"Snooze for a good cause"

Wake a Wish is a very simple to use charity alarm clock application for your smart phone. Use the alarm clock app and everytime you snooze you automatically donate money to a charity organisation of your choice.

The application acts as a platform to enable users to take part in a different way of financially contributing to non-profit organisations. The Wake a Wish application offers a fun way of donating money in order to get rid of the barriers that traditional ways of donating money often have.

In the process of creating the graphic profile for Wake a Wish the most important thing were to keep it simple and to give it a dreamy vibe. A playful, hand-drawn logotype in combination with the dark interface and the sky filled with stars as a background gives the application exactly this.
