Wake a Wish

"Do good by being late in the morning"

Wake a wish is a very simple to use alarm clock charity application. Use the alarm clock app and everytime you snooze in the morning you automatically donate money to a charity organisation of your choice.

The branding gives the user a sense of dreaming and playfulness,
